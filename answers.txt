Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Git is useful for team/group projects or in other situations where
there may be multiple different people making changes to code. It makes it easier for
multiple people to simultaneously make different changes without interfering with
eachother as much.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science can be applied to economics. An example could be get data on what
kinds of items people are buying online and in-stores, and use that data to make
predictions about how the stock market could change or just to analyze what items are
in high or low demand at the time.
